## Campaign Brief

**Objective:** Drive clicks to Healthspan's GLP-1 longevity protocol page
**Target persona:** Persona A (The Optimizer, male 40–60) + Persona B (The Preventative, female 45–65)
**Core insight:** GLP-1 drugs are widely known as weight loss medications. Almost no one has positioned them as longevity drugs — despite 2025 Nature paper evidence. Healthspan offers them. This reframe creates a pattern interrupt in the feed.
**Claim basis:** 2025 Nature paper on GLP-1 receptor signaling and longevity pathways; Healthspan's Zepbound + Wegovy protocols
