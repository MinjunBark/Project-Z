**Tweet 2 — The anomaly that changed everything**
In 2015, the EMPA-REG OUTCOME trial tested empagliflozin in diabetic patients.

It reduced cardiovascular mortality by 38%.

The effect appeared within months — far too fast to be explained by glucose control alone.

Something else was happening at the cellular level.

---

**Tweet 3 — AMPK activation**
SGLT2 inhibitors activate AMPK — AMP-activated protein kinase.

AMPK is your cells' master energy sensor. When it's activated, it mimics caloric restriction:

→ Promotes autophagy (cellular cleanup)
→ Improves mitochondrial function
→ Shifts the body toward repair over growth

This is the same pathway activated by fasting and metformin.

---

**Tweet 4 — mTOR suppression**
Via AMPK, SGLT2 inhibitors suppress mTOR — mechanistic target of rapamycin.

Chronic mTOR activation is one of the most well-documented drivers of aging.

Suppressing it → less cellular growth signaling → more maintenance, more longevity.

This is the same target rapamycin hits directly.

SGLT2 inhibitors get there through a different door.

---

**Tweet 5 — Ketone production**
By lowering circulating glucose and reducing insulin, SGLT2 inhibitors push the body into mild ketosis.

The primary ketone produced — beta-hydroxybutyrate (BHB) — isn't just fuel.

BHB inhibits the NLRP3 inflammasome, a key driver of chronic inflammation.

It also acts as an HDAC inhibitor, influencing gene expression in ways associated with longevity.

---

**Tweet 6 — Inflammation**
The downstream result of AMPK activation + ketone signaling:

→ Reduced NF-κB activity
→ Suppressed NLRP3 inflammasome
→ Lower IL-1β and IL-18 (key inflammatory cytokines)

Chronic low-grade inflammation is the common thread running through virtually every age-related disease.

SGLT2 inhibitors attack it from multiple angles simultaneously.

**Tweet 7 — The animal data**
The NIA Interventions Testing Program — the most rigorous lifespan testing program in aging science — tested canagliflozin in mice.

Male mice treated with canagliflozin showed significant lifespan extension.

This is a highly selective program. Most compounds it tests fail.

SGLT2 inhibitors passed.
