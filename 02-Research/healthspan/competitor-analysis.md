## Competitive Summary

### Healthspan's Strongest Advantages vs. Competition
- 70+ biomarkers (3x more comprehensive than most — this claim needs protecting in content)
- 12K patients + 4.9★ Trustpilot — strongest social proof in digital longevity
- Media coverage (WSJ, Washington Post) — credibility most Tier 1 competitors lack
- HSA/FSA eligible — reduces cost barrier without reducing price
- Integrated platform (labs + meds + coaching) — vs. Heally's marketplace model

### Healthspan's Vulnerabilities
- Hone Health's $65 entry undercuts Healthspan at the top of the funnel
- Longevity Direct's AI coaching + wearable is a differentiator Healthspan doesn't have
- Fella Health owns the men-only niche — Healthspan's gender-neutral positioning needs stronger women's health messaging
- Protocol's accountability model ($695/mo) shows there's a premium tier above Healthspan

### Content Strategy Implications
- SEO opportunity: most competitors don't publish scientific content at Healthspan's depth
- GEO/AEO opportunity: "Hallmarks of Aging" framework + competitor comparison content can dominate AI search
- Keyword gaps likely: Rapamycin, GLP-1 longevity (not just weight loss), SGLT2 for aging
- Social proof angle: 12K patients + Trustpilot is under-leveraged in content
