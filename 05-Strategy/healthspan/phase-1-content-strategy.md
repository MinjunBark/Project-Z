# Phase 1 Content Strategy: Healthspan
Date: 2026-05-07
Agent: Strategist (Tier 2)
Status: Draft — awaiting CEO approval

---

## Phase Objective

Establish Healthspan as the most credible educational voice in digital longevity.
Own the informational search layer — the content clinics leave uncontested —
while defending existing keyword rankings and building a compounding social presence.

**The strategic bet:** Competitors sell products. Healthspan will own the science.

---

## Phase 1 Scope

- Duration: 4 weeks (Sprint 1)
- Output: 4 long-form articles + 12 social pieces = 16 content items
- Platforms: Blog (SEO), Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn
- Primary agent: Content Writer (Tier 3)
- Visual support: Creative Prompter (Tier 3) for Instagram carousels

---

## Priority Logic

Based on keyword map and competitor analysis, Phase 1 targets three opportunity types:

| Type | Rationale | Keywords |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Uncontested informational** | No clinic ranks. Academic papers only. Healthspan can own these definitively. | hallmarks of aging, cellular senescence, mTOR cycling |
| **Early mover** | Trending topic, thin clinic content, one Healthspan article already ranking | GLP-1 for longevity (not weight loss) |
| **AEO reinforcement** | Healthspan already ranks. Restructure for AI search citation slot. | SGLT2 inhibitors for aging |

Persona weighting per piece is defined in each brief.
