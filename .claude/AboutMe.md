## Vision

social media presence end-to-end for clients.

The core pipeline:
1. Extract client brand voice from their website and existing assets
2. Build a content funnel — creatives, campaigns, blog posts
3. Implement SEO, GEO, and AEO strategies tailored to their industry
4. Run competitive analysis using custom scrapers and market research
5. Deliver consistent, high-quality marketing outcomes at scale

Build methodology: Agile. One proven step at a time. No feature is added
until the previous step is validated. Scalability, quality, and efficiency
are the criteria — not speed.

End state: an agency that takes a client brief and delivers results
with minimal manual intervention from the CEO.

6. **Agile discipline** — build one step at a time. Never jump ahead of what has been proven to work.
